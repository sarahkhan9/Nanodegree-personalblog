Barbecue chicken recipe

Barbecue chicken recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs & Drumsticks
  • Dry Rub
  • BBQ Sauce

    • Recipe

    Place the chicken on a large baking sheet and bring to room temperature. Pat dry with a paper towel. Stir the dry rub in a small bowl, sprinkle it onto each piece of chicken, then rub it all over with your hands to make sure the chicken is well coated. Don't forget to rub the spices under the skin too! Prepare one side of your grill for high, direct heat, leaving the burners off on the other side. If you are using charcoal or wood, make sure there is a cool side to the grill where there are few to no coals. Lay the chicken on the indirect heat side of the grill, yet close to the heart.
